Quarter 1 LL Contents

Basically, for the first quarter of lifelabs and english. Group 3 Premium decided on gaming reactions based on majority vote. We also researched how gaming can affect the human thought process, mainly addiction to video games. We held a survey to see what type of content the people of APEC Schools Kalumpang Grade 10 AM-PM Students would want to see us make. Things like methodology, streaming type, etc. were all done in Group 3 Premium’s Quarter 1 journey. The content chosen by the people surveyed are as follows:

  • 33 (66%) people chose Gaming videos
  • 9 (18%) people chose Podcast
  • 8 (16%) people chose Reaction videos.

    • We choose the most voted among Grade 10 AM-PM Students which is the Gaming Videos who has 66% vote. For the Question of ‘What type of platforms do you usually watch these types of contents on?’ the result is the following:

  • 45 (90%) people chose youtube
  • 35 (70%) people chose tiktok
  • 27 (54%) people chose facebook
  • 16 (32%) people chose twitch
  • 10 (20%) people chose twitter/x
  • 2 (4%) people chose instagram
  • 1 (2%) person chose aniwave.tv
  • 1 (2%) person chose bigo

    • Moreover, for the question ‘What gaming content do you usually watch?’ Grade 10 choose the following:

  • (90.0%) people chose the first-person shooter games
  • 20 (60.6%) people chose the open world games
  • 18 (54.5%) people chose the e-games
  • 16 (48.5%) people chose the horror games
  • 15 (45.4%) people chose the fighting games
  • 4 (12.1%) people chose the platform games
  • 4 (12.1%) people chose the turn-based strategy games
  • (9.1%) people chose the MMORPG

    • According to the data, 90% of grade 10 students choose “first-person shooter games” as the “gaming content they usually watch” and there is also a tie in the data which are “platform games” and “turn-based strategy games” meanwhile MMORPG is the least chosen with the data of 9.1%.
